About Me

Hi, I’m Claudia better known as Clauds. I’m that girl you’ve seen baking up those tasty donuts. I’m a Valley gal who resides in the Woodland Hills area. Believe it or not I have lived here my whole life and wouldn’t have it any other way.

I didn’t know I wanted to bake until years ago, I was a wedding intern (for a short, tiny while) and noticed I loved the dessert tables and how beautifully everything was done. I made things here and there for co workers and unfortunately didn’t have the confidence in myself to start selling.

It wasn’t until this year, during the pandemic, that I got injured and had a whooooole lot of time on my hands so I made a plan to bake something new everyday. I told myself that if I didn’t go for it now then I would never do it.
